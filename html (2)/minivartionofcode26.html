Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive and the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. He joined Google in 2004 and rose through the ranks to lead product management and innovation for a suite of Google's client software products.

Key Facts

  • Born: June 10, 1972, Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India
  • Education: B.Tech in Metallurgical Engineering from IIT Kharagpur; M.S. in Material Sciences and Engineering from Stanford University; MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
  • Joined Google: 2004
  • CEO of Google: 2015
  • CEO of Alphabet: 2019

About Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai has overseen the development of many of Google’s key products, including Chrome, Chrome OS, and Google Drive. He is known for his calm leadership style and strategic focus on artificial intelligence and cloud computing.
