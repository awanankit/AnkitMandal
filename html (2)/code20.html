Poet Sunil Gangopadhyay (সুনীল গঙ্গোপাধ্যায়)

About

Sunil Gangopadhyay was a renowned Indian Bengali poet and novelist. He is considered one of the most influential literary figures in Bengali literature.

Personal Details

  • Born: 1934 in Kolkata, West Bengal, India
  • Died: 2012
  • Language: Bengali
  • Occupation: Poet, Novelist, Journalist

Major Works

  • Poetry: "Prothom Protibeshi", "Onyo Jatra"
  • Novels: "Sonaali Swarnalata", "Kaner Dhara"
  • Short Stories: Various collections

Awards & Recognition

  • Sahitya Akademi Award
  • Padma Shri (India's fourth highest civilian award)
  • Bangla Academy Award

Legacy

Sunil Gangopadhyay's works continue to inspire generations of readers and writers in Bengal and across the world. His contribution to Bengali literature remains significant and memorable.
