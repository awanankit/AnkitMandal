Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose

Autobiography & Life

Born: January 23, 1897 in Cuttack, Odisha

Subhas Chandra Bose was an Indian nationalist leader who advocated for complete independence from British rule. He was a charismatic organizer and a powerful speaker who inspired millions of Indians.

Early Life: Bose studied in Cambridge and was influenced by nationalist ideals. He returned to India to join the independence movement.

Political Career: He served as President of the Indian National Congress and was known for his militant approach to independence.

Legacy: Netaji is remembered as a fearless leader who sought to free India through armed struggle and international support.
